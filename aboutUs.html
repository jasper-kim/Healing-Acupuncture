Our Mission

I want to be a practitioner treating not only pain in the body, but also pain in the mind

It is written that there are three kinds of practitioners in an old Traditional Chinese Medicine book.

First of all, a practitioner treating diseases of patient by understanding the mind of patients, Second of all, a practitioner treating diseases of patient by controlling the food of patients, Third of all, a practitioner treating diseases of patient by prescribing the herbal medicine to patients.

When I read above-mentioned, I thought that it would take so long time to be a practitioner treating diseases of patient by understanding the mind of patients. One day, I have asked by myself about the meaning as I treated patients everyday. I met the patient everyday and asked by myself “Don’t I think that it is too difficult to be the practitioner.” However, while I treat patients, I thought that a practitioner who is willing to listen and to try all their ears to the patient’s worry and anxiety is the practitioner treating diseases of patient by understanding the mind of patients.

I want to be a practitioner treating not only pain in the body, but also pain in the mind.
